Projects

Leauge of Legends Stat Tracker
Click the logo to go view the code
This is personally my favorite side project. I created a Player Stat Tracker for Leauge of Legends. The Tracker allows players to search up any player they want, and see their current rank in the game,
the amount of wins they have and the amount of losses they have, finally it allows them to see the win loss ratio as a percentage along with their scores from the last 5 games.
In additon, when they search for a player it shows their current profile icon and level. I created this using React.js, Javascript, and also used axios to pull all the data from the Riot games API.
Resturant app
Click the pciture to go view the code
In this group project, my team and I created a resturant application. This application offered three types of accounts a user account, manager account, and worker account.
The user account was used for customers and with our app they were able to order food, and add/remove items from their current cart as they please,
in addition it stored the users phone number which they could update aswell as their credit card information, the user could also view how long their order would take and their order number.
If the user logged in as a manager they could update the current menu aswell as update the picture that was next to the food item, the food item price, ingredients, time to make, etc.
Finally the worker log in allowed the workers at the resutrant to login and process a customers order.

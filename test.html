Leauge of Legends Stat Tracker
This is personally my favorite side project. I created a Player Stat Tracker for League of Legends. The Tracker allows players to search up any player they want, and see their current rank in the game,
the amount of wins they have and the amount of losses they have, finally it allows them to see the win-loss ratio as a percentage along with their scores from the last 5 games.
In addition, when they search for a player it shows their current profile icon and level. I created this using React.js, Javascript, and also used axios to pull all the data from the Riot games API.
Resturant app
In this group project, my team and I created a restaurant application. This application offered three types of accounts a user account, manager account, and worker account.
The user account was used for customers and with our app they were able to order food, and add/remove items from their current cart as they please,
in addition it stored the users phone number which they could update as well as their credit card information, the user could also view how long their order would take and their order number.
If the user logged in as a manager they could update the current menu as well as update the picture that was next to the food item, the food item price, ingredients, time to make, etc.
Finally the worker log in allowed the workers at the restaurant to login and process a customer's order.

Discord Name Changer Bot
I originally created this bot for my World of Warcraft guild members. The goal behind it was to easily link guild members in-game names to discord.
The reason being that a lot of people had different igns and discord names, and not everyone was following the rules to join the server.
With my bot, whenever someone joined the discord server they would receive a pm from my bot asking them their in-game name, once the bot got a response
it would update that person's server nickname to what they had entered, and then gave them permissions to access the rest of the server chat channels.
And the bot also has a change name command so players could change their server nickname using the bot if they ever changed their in-game name.
